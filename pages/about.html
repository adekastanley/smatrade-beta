Who are we

SMADAC Securities Limited is a trading license holder of the Nigeria Exchange Limited (NGX) with an authorized share capital of N1.5Billion and paid-up share capital of N382.850 Million. The Company was incorporated on September 9th 2002 and licensed on April 15th 2004. We are fully licensed to operate as Securities and Exchange Com (SEC) Broker/Dealer and Funds/Portfolio Mgr, NGX Limited Trading License Holder, FMDQ Exchange Limited Associate Member Broker, and NASD Exchange Limited Broker/Dealer.
